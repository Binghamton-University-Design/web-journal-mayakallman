Project 2: Exquisite Corpse

Made in Collaboration with Courtney Handsman and Anushna Barua

"Exquisite corpse" is a method by which a collection of words or images is collectively assembled. Each collaborator adds their own piece and at the end, the final product is a combination of all the pieces. For this project, my group decided on a common set of rule: 1. That our canvases would be 600pt high and 1300 pt wide, 2. That we would use a restricted color palette that we had agreed on, and 3. That our illustrations would be mythology themed.

The head was designed by Anushna, the mid section was designed by Courtney, and I designed the tail. This project was a fun exercise in creativity and coordination between group members
